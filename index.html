Where to Stay

We have room blocks at three hotels. The Colony and The Brazilian Court are both located in Palm Beach. AKA West Palm is a short drive across the bridge.

The Venue Hotel · Palm Beach
The Colony Palm Beach

A Palm Beach icon, The Colony is the backdrop for our wedding. Recognizable for its signature pink façade, iconic interiors, and timeless glamour — The Colony is where our wedding day takes place.

To book, please call the hotel and reference the Knowles–Crepault wedding room block for the room block rate. Rooms are available on a first-come, first-served basis — we recommend booking early.

Getting There
Palm Beach International (PBI)

The closest and most convenient airport — just a 15-minute drive to the hotels. We'd recommend flying into PBI if you can. Once you land, Uber, Lyft, and taxis are all available.

Fort Lauderdale (FLL) & Miami (MIA)

Fort Lauderdale is about an hour's drive north; Miami is roughly 1 hr 15 min to the south. From either airport, Uber and Lyft are your easiest options into Palm Beach.

Getting Around

Palm Beach island is a very walkable town — guests at The Colony and The Brazilian Court can walk between the two hotels and along Worth Avenue with ease. For everything else, Uber and Lyft work reliably throughout the area.

If you prefer a local cab, here are a few numbers to keep handy:

  • Yellow Cab — (561) 777-7777
  • East Coast Taxi — (561) 651-1111
  • West Palm Beach Taxi — (561) 371-7171

Please note — there is no dedicated shuttle service between the hotels and the venue — but with the distances involved, getting around is easy.
